The Unhappy Afghanistan

This year, Afghanistan is by far the least happy country, underperforming the second lowest ranking country, Zimbabwe by almost 20% and its regional average by 43%. Afghanistan also scored less than half the global average.

Finland is once again the happiest country in the world

Finland remains the happiest country in the world for the fourth consecutive year since it was crowned the happiest country in the world in 2018.
